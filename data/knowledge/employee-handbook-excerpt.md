# Delaware Valley Paving — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **Year 1–3:** 15 days PTO + 6 paid holidays
- **Year 4–9:** 20 days PTO + 6 paid holidays
- **Year 10+:** 25 days PTO + 6 paid holidays
- PTO requests must be submitted via the HR portal 2 weeks in advance. Requests during peak paving season (April–November) require supervisor approval.

## Safety Commitment
DVP maintains a zero-tolerance policy for unsafe behavior. All field employees must complete OSHA 10-hour Construction within 90 days of hire. Foremen and superintendents must hold OSHA 30-hour certification. Safety violations result in immediate written warning; a second violation within 12 months triggers a 3-day suspension.

## Anti-Harassment & Discrimination
DVP is an equal-opportunity employer. Harassment or discrimination based on race, gender, religion, national origin, age, disability, or sexual orientation is prohibited. Reports can be made to HR Director Lisa Connors at hr@dvpaving.com or via the anonymous hotline at 1-800-555-0199.

## Drug & Alcohol Policy
All field employees are subject to pre-employment, random, post-accident, and reasonable-suspicion drug testing. CDL holders must comply with DOT drug and alcohol testing requirements (49 CFR Part 40). A positive result results in immediate removal from safety-sensitive duties and referral to the Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

## Dress Code
- **Field:** Hard hat, high-visibility vest (ANSI Class 2 minimum), steel-toe boots, long pants. No loose clothing near equipment.
- **Office:** Business casual. Steel-toe boots required when visiting any job site or the yard.
