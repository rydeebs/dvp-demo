# DVP Safety Procedures Manual

## Work Zone Setup (MUTCD Compliant)
1. Deploy advance warning signs (W20-1 "Road Work Ahead") at least 500 ft before taper on roads ≥40 mph.
2. Place channelizing devices (28" cones or Type III barricades) at 40 ft spacing for the taper.
3. Install arrow boards in flashing or sequential mode for lane closures.
4. All flaggers must hold current ATSSA or state flagger certification.
5. Shadow vehicle with TMA required on all state highway work.

## Hot Mix Asphalt Handling
- HMA delivery temperature: 275°F–325°F. Reject loads below 250°F or above 350°F.
- PPE required: heat-resistant gloves, long sleeves, safety glasses, steel-toe boots.
- Never stand in front of the paver hopper during material transfer.
- Tack coat application: ban open flames within 25 ft of tack truck operations.
- Release agents: use approved non-petroleum-based release agents only (diesel fuel is prohibited).

## Confined Space Entry
- Applies to manholes, utility vaults, and deep excavations (>4 ft).
- Requires: atmospheric testing (O₂, LEL, H₂S, CO), entry permit signed by competent person, dedicated attendant, rescue plan.
- DVP uses the MSA Altair 5X multi-gas detector. Units must be bump-tested daily.

## Fall Protection
- Required at 6 ft above lower level (OSHA 1926.501).
- Bridge deck and elevated structure work: personal fall arrest system (harness + lanyard + anchor rated to 5,000 lbs).
- Leading edge paving on bridge approaches: use warning line system at 15 ft from edge minimum.

## Incident Reporting
All incidents (injury, near-miss, property damage, vehicle accident) must be reported to the foreman within 1 hour and documented on DVP Form SA-100 within 24 hours. The Safety Director (Dan Murphy, x4401) reviews all reports weekly.
