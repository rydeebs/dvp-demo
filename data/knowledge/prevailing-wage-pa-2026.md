# Pennsylvania Prevailing Wage Rates — 2026
**Effective:** January 1, 2026 | **Source:** PA Dept. of Labor & Industry

Rates below apply to highway/heavy construction in Chester, Delaware, and Montgomery counties.

## Foremen
Foremen receive base rate of the highest-classified worker supervised plus $2.00/hr premium.

## Overtime
- Time and a half after 8 hours/day and on Saturdays.
- Double time on Sundays and holidays.

*Note: These rates apply to publicly funded projects exceeding $25,000. Private work uses DVP's standard crew rates.*
